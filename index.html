    • Let's Go Shopping

    If only this existed in real life...

    Project maintained by JordanHamel Hosted on GitHub Pages — Theme by mattgraham

    I'll admit it, I love going shopping. Even a trip to the grocery store gets me excited. So I thought to myself, what would the world's best shopping trip be like?

    Baby animals.

    Since I don't own a zoo, or even a pet store, I built this site (along with @SarahKetabchi) where you can pretend to buy all the cute baby animals your heart desires.
